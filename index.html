Quick Guide to Teddy's Currency Converter

Using Teddy's Currency Converter is simple and easy!

In the topmost row, choose your current currency and type the amount that you want to have converted.

In the second row, choose the currency that you want to convert into.

Once you have typed the amount and chosen the correct currencies, simply click Convert or press Enter to convert your money!

Do you want to switch the currencies? Simply press Switch.

If you want to add more currencies, just press the "add" + button. You may add up to a maximum of five (5) currencies. And if you want to subtract currencies, just press the "minus" - button beside it.

Finally, to clear all values and return to zero, press Clear.
